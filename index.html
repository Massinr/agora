Our Mission

We are excited to announce the founding of a brand-new club that you can join to discuss everything school-related! From your own special interests to the inner workings of the establishment surrounding you, we will aim to create a safe space in which you can share your ideas and hobbies with peers and teachers alike and help create the ideal environment for students to thrive in.

To achieve this goal of ours we plan to host weekly or bi-weekly gatherings on school grounds where members will be able to present a topic of their choosing and receive feedback from classmates.

Discussion Topics Include:

  • Environmental issues within our city, how to properly maintain our school, community service projects
  • Recent research and breakthroughs in any scientific field, emerging technology
  • School policy, students' needs and concerns
